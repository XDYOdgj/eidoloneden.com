Beautiful Burnt Orange Silk Flowers

Add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your special day with these stunning burnt orange silk flowers. Handcrafted from 90% premium silk and 10% durable plastic, they offer a lifelike appearance that enhances the look of any dessert or event setting. These flowers are perfectly suited for weddings, birthdays, and anniversaries, blending beauty and practicality in one elegant package.

Each set includes three flower pieces and six laser butterfly accents to help you create a full and eye-catching design. The convenient handles and double-sided tapes make installation and customization effortless, allowing you to experiment with different placements and styles. These reusable floral arrangements require no watering, no wilting, and no maintenance, making them an ideal choice for any occasion where beauty and convenience are key.
