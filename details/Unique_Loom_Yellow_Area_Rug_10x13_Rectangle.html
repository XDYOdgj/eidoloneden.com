Unique Loom Solo Collection Yellow Area Rug

Transform your space with the Unique Loom Solo Collection Yellow Area Rug, a perfect blend of durability and design. Crafted from high-quality polypropylene pile, this rug delivers a soft yet robust feel that withstands everyday traffic and remains looking great for years to come.

The machine-made design ensures uniform quality, while the cotton backing adds comfort and stability underfoot. It offers reliable adhesion to floors when paired with an underlay, making it safe and secure for all areas of your home.

Its vibrant yellow color instantly brightens any room, bringing warmth and energy to living areas or entryways. Ideal for families and pet owners, the stain-resistant surface and easy maintenance make it a low-effort, high-impact home decor choice.

Whether you're searching for a statement piece or a practical floor covering, this 10' x 13' rectangle rug is built to last and designed to impress. Keep it fresh with simple spot cleaning and vacuuming (without the beater bar).
